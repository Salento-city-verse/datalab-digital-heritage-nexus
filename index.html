Welcome to Digital Heritage Nexus

Our platform aims to revolutionize cultural tourism by providing immersive, sustainable, and engaging experiences. Through the integration of advanced technologies such as VR, AR, and IoT, we aim to preserve cultural landmarks and optimize visitor experiences while minimizing environmental impact.

Explore Our Platform Features

Discover how Digital Heritage Nexus can transform your tourism experience and help preserve Salento’s cultural heritage. Our platform integrates cutting-edge digital technologies to enhance your understanding and connection with Salento's rich cultural assets while promoting environmental sustainability.

  • The Immersive Engagement Layer leverages advanced technologies like 3D digital twins, virtual reality (VR), and augmented reality (AR) to offer visitors an interactive and educational experience. Explore high-fidelity 3D models of Salento’s cultural heritage sites, and engage with AI-driven simulations and historical reconstructions, making heritage exploration more meaningful and immersive.

  • The Sustainability Optimization Layer uses AI-powered analytics, IoT sensors, and crowd management tools to ensure responsible tourism. Predictive crowd management helps mitigate overtourism, while IoT sensors monitor environmental conditions to optimize resource usage, minimizing the impact on Salento’s cultural landmarks and ecosystem.
